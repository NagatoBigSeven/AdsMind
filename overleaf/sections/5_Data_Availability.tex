All agent-side benchmark inputs, frozen configurations, runner scripts, and curated structured outputs used in this study are stored in the repository under \texttt{research/agent\_eval/} and \texttt{research/results/}. The public repository keeps the paper-facing summary tables, backend-comparison artifacts, and LaTeX table exports used in the manuscript, while per-run logs, trajectories, and structure artifacts are treated as regenerable execution products. The reported benchmark and ablation summaries can therefore be reproduced from the archived manifests, frozen configurations, and runner scripts.

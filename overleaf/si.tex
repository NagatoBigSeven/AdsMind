The original locked10 four-backend OCD package is retained below as the first complete-energy mechanism set.
The current formal-ready expansion uses 24 OCD cases across GPT-5.4, Claude~Sonnet~4.6, and Gemini~2.5~Pro only; Grok-4 extra14 and Grok-4 rep50 Full are excluded from this snapshot because the relevant batches were not complete clean matrices at the analysis freeze owing to external service failures.
The 24-case formal table is assembled from the audited locked10 subset (003, 004, 005, 012, 013, 015, 016, 019, 023, 024) plus the extra14 extension (001, 002, 006, 007, 008, 009, 010, 011, 014, 017, 018, 020, 021, 022).

The completed additions are the CMU20 five-variant ablation completion, CMU20 random placement, MACE-MP-0 large sensitivity on CMU20 and OCD-rep10, and an Adsorb-Agent single-configuration stress control.

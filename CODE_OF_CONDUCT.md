# Code of Conduct

AdsMind is a research software project. We expect contributors and maintainers
to keep discussions professional, constructive, and welcoming.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful when reviewing code, data, methods, and scientific claims.
- Assume good intent while asking for evidence and reproducibility.
- Keep feedback specific, actionable, and tied to the work.
- Credit prior work and disclose relevant limitations.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private communication, credentials, or personal information without
  permission.
- Repeated off-topic disruption of issues, pull requests, or discussions.
- Misrepresenting benchmark results, provenance, authorship, or licensing.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; reject pull
requests; or block participants when behavior harms the project community.
Please report concerns privately to the maintainers listed in the repository
metadata.
